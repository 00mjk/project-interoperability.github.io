Project Interoperability consolidates principles and authorities in numerous presidential directives, policy documents, strategies, and agency guidelines, notably:

* [The Intelligence Reform and Terrorism Prevention Act of 2004](http://ise.gov/intelligence-reform-and-terrorism-prevention-act-2004-sec-1016-information-sharing)
* [The National Strategy for Information Sharing and Safeguarding (December 2012](http://www.whitehouse.gov/the-press-office/2012/12/19/national-strategy-information-sharing-and-safeguarding)
* [Executive Order 13587: Structural Reforms to Improve the Security of Classified Networks and the Responsible Sharing and Safeguarding of Classified Information](http://www.whitehouse.gov/the-press-office/2011/10/07/executive-order-structural-reforms-improve-security-classified-networks-)
* [Executive Order 13642: Making Open and Machine Readable the New Default for Government Information](http://www.whitehouse.gov/the-press-office/2013/05/09/executive-order-making-open-and-machine-readable-new-default-government-)
* [Executive Office of the President, The Federal Information Technology Shared Services Strategy (May 2012](http://www.whitehouse.gov/blog/2012/05/02/introducing-it-shared-services-strategy)
* [Open Government Directive (December 2009)](http://www.whitehouse.gov/sites/default/files/omb/assets/memoranda_2010/m10-06.pdf)
* [Presidential Memorandum, The Digital Government: Building a 21st Century Platform to Better Serve the American People (May 2012)](http://www.whitehouse.gov/the-press-office/2012/05/23/presidential-memorandum-building-21st-century-digital-government)
* [Executive Office of the President, The Common Approach to Federal Enterprise Architecture (May 2012)](http://www.whitehouse.gov/omb/e-gov/fea)
* [National Institute of Standards and Technology, A Credential Reliability and Revocation Model for Federated Identities (Nov. 2012)](http://nvlpubs.nist.gov/nistpubs/ir/2012/NIST.IR.7817.pdf)
